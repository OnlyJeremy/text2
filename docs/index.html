Elara Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2025

Elara222 ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Elara mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Hand gesture data processed locally for real-time interaction (not stored)
  • Camera access to enable AR functionality (video is not recorded or transmitted)
  • Device information such as OS version and model, for app performance and compatibility
  • Optional usage analytics to understand how users interact with the App (anonymous and aggregated)

We do not collect personal identifiers like name, email, or location.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Enable real-time AR drawing based on hand movements
  • Improve app features and usability
  • Ensure proper functioning on supported devices
  • Monitor and troubleshoot technical issues

3. Data Storage and Sharing

  • No personal data is stored on our servers
  • All hand and camera input is processed on-device
  • We do not sell or share any user data with third parties
  • Anonymous analytics, if enabled, may be processed by trusted partners like Firebase or Apple services

4. Your Rights and Controls

  • You may disable camera access at any time in your device settings
  • You can opt out of anonymous analytics via your device’s system settings
  • You may delete the App at any time to stop all data collection

5. Children’s Privacy

Elara is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect or store data from anyone under the age of 18.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. Material changes will be communicated through the App or on our website.
